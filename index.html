Creative Child

Creative Child School came to Zeenspier with a strong vision, which was to create a truly inspiring website to front their online communications, demonstrating their core values.

The core purpose of the website is to attract prospective parents to the prestigious private school located in the heart of Bengaluru. In doing so, the website content focuses on the values of the school's culture, relationships and teaching.

The website also incorporates other user focussed features:

  • Latest news functionality
  • School goals and Culture
  • Staff Details
  • Google maps integration
  • Contact form

ICAT - Informed Choice After Tenth

ICAT broke the mould with their project brief and asked us to create a bright, all in one, modern website to promote what they usually do to students - Awareness.

The core purpose of the website is to let the students know what to pursue after tenth. The webiste is a one for all place to register for seminars, read testominals from others and show representative tips.

The website also incorporates other user focussed features:

  • Latest news functionality
  • School goals and Culture
  • Schedules/ Calendar
  • Registration
  • Representative Images
